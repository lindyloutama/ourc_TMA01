ABOUT THE RUNNING CLUB

The Club is run on a rather informal basis in that there are no fixed meeting times or formally arranged groups, or club fees!
Runners usually run at lunchtimes and meet each other by informal arrangement at the pavilion.
In general there is a group (mainly men) that leaves for a run at about 12.20pm and then other groups (mainly women) that leave between 12.30pm and 12.45pm.

OURC FUN RUN

From time to time there are more organized training sessions to do fartlek or interval training.
Each month a handicap race is arranged so that runners can compete with one another.
Races are either 5 miles, 10km or 5km.
